Excel – Fashion Retail Sales Dashboard

Tools Used:

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Pivot Charts

Slicers

KPI Metrics

Project Objective:
To analyze retail sales data and identify monthly trends, top-performing products, customer satisfaction levels, and payment behavior.

Key Insights:

December recorded the highest sales indicating strong year-end demand.

Flip-Flops and Jeans are top revenue-generating products.

Average customer rating is 2.98, slightly below average.

Payment preference is evenly split between Cash and Credit Card.
